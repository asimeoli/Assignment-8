Biography

Fleetwood Mac are a British-American rock band, formed in London in 1967. The band has sold more than 100 million records worldwide, making them one of the world's best-selling bands of all time.

The band has had a turbulent history since its founding, members have come and gone (sometimes more than once.) Fleetwood Mac has been both a Blues-Rock and Pop-Rock band.
